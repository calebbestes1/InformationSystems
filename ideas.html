The Three Pillars of Eternity

A guide to matching your needs in familial, platonic, and romantic relationships

Shared Values

Possibly the most important thing to look for in a spouse. This may include your religion, ability i to have meaningful conversations with one another, political views, or standards.

Shared Interests

An attribute that may often be over-looked, your ability to have fun and de-stress with a spouse may go a long way.

Compatible Personalities

Notice that it's 'compatible personalities' not 'shared personalities'. This is the most subjective and hardest one to line up at times. Under compatible personalities you'll find understanding one another's humor, ability to fill each other's social needs whether you're an introvert or an extrovert, among many other factors.
